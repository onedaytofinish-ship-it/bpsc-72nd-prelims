National Appointments
📂 Current Affairs & Polity ⚖️ Weight: 1.7 📊 PYQ Count Mapped: 12 🎯 Expected: 3-5 Qs
📚 Core Content & PYQ Alignment
69th BPSC PYQ Supreme Court Collegium System: Questions tested the structural features of the collegium. It is a 5-member body headed by the incumbent Chief Justice of India (CJI) and comprises 4 other senior-most judges of the SC. High Court and SC judges are appointed exclusively via this system (Art. 124 & 217).
70th BPSC 13 Dec PYQ Supreme Court Judge Elevations: Questions focused on the specific background of newly appointed judges. For example, before his elevation to the SC in July 2024, Justice N. Kotiswar Singh served as the Chief Justice of the High Court of Jammu & Kashmir and Ladakh.
70th BPSC 4 Jan PYQ GST Council Chairperson: The constitution of bodies is regularly tested. Under Article 279A, the Chairperson of the GST Council is the Union Finance Minister, not the RBI Governor or the Finance Commission Chairman.

📊 Appointments Authority & Process Flows

Supreme Court Collegium & Appointment Chain 🔗

1. Retiring CJI initiates proposal 30 days prior
2. SC Collegium (CJI + 4 senior-most Judges)
Consults & seeks written opinion
3. Law Minister forwards recommendation to PM
4. President signs Appointment Warrant (Art. 124)

CEC Appointment Mechanism (Per 2023 Act) 🔗

Search Committee (Headed by Cabinet Secretary)
Prepares panel of 5 candidates
Selection Committee (PM + LoP + Nominated Cabinet Minister)
Recommends name to President (replaces CJI)
President appoints CEC & ECs (Art. 324)
📋 Fact Matrix — Statutory vs. Constitutional Posts
Post Type Key Office Holders Constitutional Provisions / Acts
Constitutional CJI (Art. 124), CAG (Art. 148), AG (Art. 76), CEC (Art. 324), FC Chairman (Art. 280) Created directly by the text of the Constitution of India. Highly secured tenures.
Statutory NHRC Chairperson, CVC, RBI Governor (RBI Act 1934), SEBI Chairman (SEBI Act 1992) Created by an Act of Parliament. Terms regulated by specific statutes.
Executive NSA, NITI Aayog CEO, Cabinet Secretary Established via executive resolutions. No legislative or constitutional mandate.
The Parliament of India, where statutory appointment acts (like the CEC Act 2023) are debated and enacted.

📰 Current Relevance & Potential Traps
  • The 2023 CEC Law Shift: The Supreme Court in 2023 directed that CEC should be appointed by PM + LoP + CJI. However, Parliament passed an Act replacing CJI with a nominated Cabinet Minister. BPSC frequently drafts statement questions on this specific legal sequence.
  • Differentiate Re-appointments: R. Venkataramani (AG) was extended in Oct 2025, and Tushar Mehta (SG) was extended in July 2026. Keep their tenures distinct.

✍️ MCQ Practice Set — 25 Questions (BPSC Level)
Q1. Consider the following statements regarding the Supreme Court Collegium system:
1. It is a five-member body headed by the incumbent Chief Justice of India.
2. It comprises four other senior-most judges of the Supreme Court.
3. The collegium system is explicitly mentioned in Part V of the Constitution of India.
Which of the statements given above is/are correct?
✅ Correct Answer: (B) — The Collegium is a 5-member body (CJI + 4 senior-most judges). It is a product of judicial decisions (Judges Cases) and is NOT mentioned in the Constitution text. (69th BPSC PYQ Theme)
Q2. Prior to his elevation as a judge of the Supreme Court of India in July 2024, Justice N. Kotiswar Singh was serving as the Chief Justice of which High Court?
✅ Correct Answer: (C) — Justice N. Kotiswar Singh was the Chief Justice of Jammu & Kashmir and Ladakh High Court before his elevation to the SC. (70th BPSC PYQ)
Q3. Who is the ex-officio Chairperson of the Goods and Services Tax (GST) Council?
✅ Correct Answer: (B) — Article 279A states that the Union Finance Minister acts as the Chairperson of the GST Council. (70th BPSC PYQ)
Q4. Under the Chief Election Commissioner and other Election Commissioners Act, 2023, who is the third member of the Selection Committee besides the Prime Minister and the Leader of the Opposition?
✅ Correct Answer: (B) — The Selection Committee comprises the PM (Chairperson), the Leader of the Opposition in Lok Sabha, and a Union Cabinet Minister nominated by the PM. The CJI was excluded by the 2023 Act.
Q5. Who is the 26th Governor of the Reserve Bank of India, appointed in December 2024?
✅ Correct Answer: (A) — Sanjay Malhotra (former Revenue Secretary) was appointed as the 26th Governor of the RBI on Dec 11, 2024.
Q6. Who became the first BJP Chief Minister of Bihar in April 2026?
✅ Correct Answer: (B) — Samrat Choudhary assumed office as the Chief Minister of Bihar on April 15, 2026, heading a BJP-led government.
Q7. Who is the 53rd Chief Justice of India, appointed in November 2025?
✅ Correct Answer: (B) — Justice Surya Kant took oath as the 53rd CJI on November 24, 2025, succeeding Justice Sanjiv Khanna.
Q8. Consider the following statements regarding the Attorney General for India:
1. The Attorney General is appointed by the President under Article 76 of the Constitution.
2. R. Venkataramani was re-appointed for a fresh term in October 2025.
3. The Attorney General has a fixed constitutional tenure of 5 years.
Which of the statements given above are correct?
✅ Correct Answer: (A) — Statement 3 is incorrect. The AG holds office during the pleasure of the President and has no fixed tenure under Article 76.
Q9. The current Chief of Defence Staff (CDS) of India, appointed in May 2026, is:
✅ Correct Answer: (B) — Gen. N. S. Raja Subramani was appointed as the 3rd CDS of India on May 31, 2026.
Q10. Who became the Governor of Bihar in March 2026, succeeding Rajendra Arlekar?
✅ Correct Answer: (B) — Lt. Gen. (Retd.) Syed Ata Hasnain took oath as Governor of Bihar on March 14, 2026.
Q11. Match the following Constitutional office holders with their corresponding Articles:
a) Comptroller and Auditor General — 1) Article 76
b) Attorney General — 2) Article 324
c) Chief Election Commissioner — 3) Article 148
d) Governor — 4) Article 155
✅ Correct Answer: (A) — CAG is Article 148, AG is Article 76, CEC/ECs are Article 324, and Governor's appointment is Article 155.
Q12. Who is the current Chairman of the Union Public Service Commission (UPSC), appointed in mid-2024?
✅ Correct Answer: (B) — Preeti Sudan (former Union Health Secretary) assumed charge as UPSC Chairperson in mid-2024.
Q13. Who serves as the Chairperson of the 16th Finance Commission, constituted to recommend devolution formulas starting 2026?
✅ Correct Answer: (B) — Dr. Arvind Panagariya (economist & former Vice-Chair of NITI Aayog) heads the 16th Finance Commission.
Q14. The 14th Comptroller and Auditor General (CAG) of India, who took charge in November 2024, is:
✅ Correct Answer: (B) — K. Sanjay Murthy (former Higher Education Secretary) was sworn in as 14th CAG on Nov 21, 2024, succeeding G.C. Murmu.
Q15. Who was the first Chief Election Commissioner (CEC) of India?
✅ Correct Answer: (B) — Sukumar Sen served as the first CEC of India from 1950 to 1958.
Q16. The ex-officio Chairman of the Rajya Sabha is:
✅ Correct Answer: (B) — Under Article 64, the Vice President of India acts as the ex-officio Chairman of the Council of States (Rajya Sabha).
Q17. The new Chief of the Army Staff (COAS), appointed on June 30, 2026, is:
✅ Correct Answer: (C) — Gen. Dhiraj Seth assumed charge as COAS on June 30, 2026.
Q18. Who was appointed as the Chief Secretary of Bihar in September 2025?
✅ Correct Answer: (B) — Pratyay Amrit (IAS 1991) assumed charge as Chief Secretary of Bihar on September 1, 2025.
Q19. Who decides the disqualification of members of a State Legislative Assembly for matters other than the Tenth Schedule?
✅ Correct Answer: (B) — Under Article 192, questions of disqualification (other than defection) are decided by the Governor, whose decision is final after obtaining the opinion of the EC. (70th BPSC PYQ)
Q20. Who is the first President of India from Bihar who served two consecutive terms?
✅ Correct Answer: (B) — Dr. Rajendra Prasad (born in Siwan, Bihar) was the first President of India and served two consecutive terms (1950–1962).
Q21. The Solicitor General of India, extended for 3 years in July 2026, is:
✅ Correct Answer: (B) — Tushar Mehta has been Solicitor General since 2018 and received another extension in July 2026.
Q22. Who is the CEO of NITI Aayog (as of July 2026)?
✅ Correct Answer: (B) — B.V.R. Subrahmanyam serves as the Chief Executive Officer (CEO) of NITI Aayog. Suman Bery is the Vice-Chairperson.
Q23. Who was appointed as the Chief of the Naval Staff (CNS) on May 31, 2026?
✅ Correct Answer: (B) — Admiral Krishna Swaminathan was appointed as the new CNS on May 31, 2026.
Q24. Who was the Chief Minister of Bihar in March 1939?
✅ Correct Answer: (B) — Shri Krishna Singh headed the first Congress ministry in Bihar and was Chief Minister (Prime Minister of Bihar province) during this period. (71st BPSC PYQ)
Q25. Who is the current Chairperson of the Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI), appointed in March 2025?
✅ Correct Answer: (B) — Tuhin Kanta Pandey replaced Madhabi Puri Buch as SEBI Chairperson on March 1, 2025.
