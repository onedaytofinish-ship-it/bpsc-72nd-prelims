Q1: PM-KISAN provides ₹6,000/year in 3 equal installments of ₹2,000 each to all landholding farmer families.

Q2: MUDRA has only 3 categories — Shishu, Kishore, Tarun. "Yuva" is not a MUDRA category.

Q3: PMSBY premium is ₹20/year. Don't confuse with PMJJBY (₹436/year).

Q4: In 2024, Ayushman Bharat was expanded to cover all senior citizens aged 70+ regardless of income status.

Q5: Swachh Bharat Mission (Rural) is under Ministry of Jal Shakti; SBM (Urban) is under MoHUA. Common exam trap!

Q6: PMAY-G: ₹1,20,000 for plains; ₹1,30,000 for hilly/difficult areas.

Q7: Mukhyamantri Kanya Utthan Yojana is Bihar's scheme providing ₹54,100 total from birth to graduation.

Q8: Namo Drone Didi (2024) provides drones to 15,000 women SHGs for agricultural spraying and monitoring.

Q9: PM-KISAN is a Central Sector Scheme (100% Centre funded). MGNREGA, NHM are Centrally Sponsored.

Q10: Jal Jeevan Mission's tagline: "Har Ghar Jal" — functional tap water to every rural household.

Q14: Bihar Student Credit Card: Up to ₹4 Lakh loan for students who have passed 12th for higher education.

Q16: National Green Hydrogen Mission: 5 MMT annual green H₂ production target by 2030; ₹19,744 Cr outlay.

Q20: PMJDY was launched on Aug 28, 2014. PM-KISAN = 2019; Ayushman = 2018; Ujjwala = 2016.
