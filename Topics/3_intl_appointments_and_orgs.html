✍js MCQ Practice Set — 25 Questions (BPSC Level)
Q1. In April 2024, a resolution to recommend admitting Palestine as a full member of the United Nations was vetoed by the United States in the 15-member UN Security Council. How many members of the council either rejected or abstained from voting?
✅ Correct Answer: (C) — 12 members voted in favor, 1 against (US veto), and 2 abstained (UK, Switzerland). Thus, a total of 3 members rejected or abstained. (70th BPSC PYQ)
Q2. Who succeeded Ruchira Kamboj as India's Permanent Representative to the United Nations in New York in September 2024?
✅ Correct Answer: (B) — Parvathaneni Harish assumed charge as India's Permanent Representative to the UN on September 1, 2024, succeeding Ruchira Kamboj who retired in June 2024.
Q3. The Global Gender Gap Report, a frequent subject in BPSC, is released annually by which organization?
✅ Correct Answer: (C) — The Global Gender Gap Report is published by the World Economic Forum. (69th BPSC PYQ)
Q4. What was the theme of the International Olympic Day celebrated on 23rd June 2024?
✅ Correct Answer: (C) — The theme of the International Olympic Day 2024 was "Let's Move and Celebrate". (70th BPSC PYQ)
Q5. Which country became the 32nd member state of NATO in March 2024?
✅ Correct Answer: (B) — Sweden officially joined NATO as its 32nd member on March 7, 2024. Finland joined as the 31st member in April 2023.
Q6. Which country became the 10th full member state of the Shanghai Cooperation Organisation (SCO) at the Astana Summit in July 2024?
✅ Correct Answer: (B) — Belarus officially became the 10th member of the SCO on July 4, 2024. Iran was admitted as the 9th member in 2023.
Q7. Who assumed office as the Secretary General of NATO on October 1, 2024, succeeding Jens Stoltenberg?
✅ Correct Answer: (A) — Mark Rutte, the former Prime Minister of the Netherlands, assumed office as NATO Secretary General on October 1, 2024.
Q8. Who is the current President of the 80th session of the United Nations General Assembly (UNGA), running from September 2025 to September 2026?
✅ Correct Answer: (C) — Annalena Baerbock (Germany) is the President of the 80th session. Dennis Francis headed the 78th session, and Philemon Yang headed the 79th session.
Q9. Which five countries officially joined BRICS as permanent members on January 1, 2024?
✅ Correct Answer: (A) — Egypt, Ethiopia, Iran, Saudi Arabia, and the UAE joined BRICS on Jan 1, 2024. Argentina was invited but declined.
Q10. Who assumed office as the Director General of the International Solar Alliance (ISA) on March 17, 2025?
✅ Correct Answer: (B) — Ashish Khanna took charge as the 3rd DG of the ISA on March 17, 2025, succeeding Dr. Ajay Mathur.
Q11. Which of the following groups of countries were elected as non-permanent members of the UN Security Council for the 2025–2026 term?
✅ Correct Answer: (A) — Denmark, Greece, Pakistan, Panama, and Somalia serve the 2025-2026 term. Bahrain, Colombia, DRC, Latvia, and Liberia serve the 2026-2027 term.
Q12. In the 2024-2025 period, who serves as the President of the International Court of Justice (ICJ)?
✅ Correct Answer: (B) — Nawaf Salam (Lebanon) was elected President of the ICJ in Feb 2024. Julia Sebutinde (Uganda) is the Vice-President.
Q13. Who is the current Managing Director of the International Monetary Fund (IMF), re-appointed for a second 5-year term starting October 2024?
✅ Correct Answer: (B) — Kristalina Georgieva (Bulgaria) was approved for a second 5-year term as MD of the IMF, starting October 1, 2024.
Q14. The 14th President of the World Bank Group is:
✅ Correct Answer: (B) — Ajay Banga (Indian-American) assumed charge as World Bank President on June 2, 2023.
Q15. Who is the Director-General of the World Trade Organization (WTO), serving a second term starting September 2025?
✅ Correct Answer: (B) — Dr. Ngozi Okonjo-Iweala of Nigeria began her second 4-year term as WTO DG on September 1, 2025. She is the first woman and first African to lead the WTO.
Q16. Who is the first Brazilian to be elected as the Secretary General of INTERPOL, taking charge in late 2024?
✅ Correct Answer: (B) — Valdecy Urquiza was elected to head INTERPOL, succeeding Germany's Jürgen Stock. He is the first head of INTERPOL from a developing nation.
Q17. Which organization admitted the African Union as a permanent member during its summit in New Delhi in September 2023?
✅ Correct Answer: (B) — The G20 admitted the African Union as a permanent member, effectively turning the group into G21.
Q18. The President of the New Development Bank (NDB), established by BRICS, is:
✅ Correct Answer: (B) — Dilma Rousseff, former President of Brazil, heads the NDB. NDB is headquartered in Shanghai.
Q19. Consider the following statements about the UN Security Council:
1. It consists of 15 members, of which 5 are permanent and 10 are non-permanent.
2. Non-permanent members are elected for a term of three years by the General Assembly.
3. Decisions on procedural matters require the affirmative vote of at least 9 members.
Which of the statements given above is/are correct?
✅ Correct Answer: (B) — Statement 2 is incorrect. Non-permanent members are elected for a term of two years, not three.
Q20. Who won the Amal Clooney Women's Empowerment Award in May 2024 from King Charles III?
✅ Correct Answer: (B) — Arti, an E-rickshaw driver from Uttar Pradesh, was conferred this award for promoting women's safety and mobility. (70th BPSC PYQ)
Q21. The head of Russia's Wagner Mercenary Group, who died in a plane crash in 2023, was:
✅ Correct Answer: (B) — Yevgeny Prigozhin was the head of the Wagner Group. (69th BPSC PYQ)
Q22. The winner of the prestigious Goldman Environmental Prize 2024 from the region of Asia is:
✅ Correct Answer: (A) — Alok Shukla won the prize for his campaign to save the Hasdeo Arand forests in Chhattisgarh, India. (70th BPSC PYQ)
Q23. Which of the following organizations is headquartered in Manila, Philippines?
✅ Correct Answer: (B) — The ADB (headed by Masatsugu Asakawa) is headquartered in Mandaluyong, Manila, Philippines. NDB is in Shanghai, and AIIB is in Beijing.
Q24. Who was the first female Indian Ambassador to the United Nations, who retired in June 2024?
✅ Correct Answer: (A) — Ruchira Kamboj was India's first woman Permanent Representative to the UN. Note: Vijaya Lakshmi Pandit was the first woman President of the UN General Assembly (1953), but Kamboj was the first female PR. (70th BPSC PYQ)
Q25. Argentina was formally invited to join BRICS at the 2023 Johannesburg Summit. However, it declined the invitation. Who is the current President of Argentina who made this decision?
✅ Correct Answer: (B) — President Javier Milei withdrew Argentina from joining BRICS upon taking office, citing shifts in foreign policy.
