7. Statics You Must Not Confuse

  • Agni ladder: I–700, II–2,000, III–3,000, IV–4,000, V–5,000+ km. Agni-P sits between Agni-I and Agni-II (1,000–2,000 km).
  • Cruise vs ballistic: BrahMos & Nirbhay fly like aircraft (cruise); Agni/Prithvi follow ballistic arcs. Pralay/Shaurya are quasi-ballistic (flatter, manoeuvring trajectory).
  • Supersonic vs hypersonic: BrahMos = supersonic (Mach ~3); ET-LDHCM = hypersonic (Mach 5+, scramjet).
  • Who uses what: Agni/Prithvi = Strategic Forces Command; BrahMos/Akash/Astra/Pinaka = all three services in different variants; VSHORADS/MPATGM = Army & IAF point defence.
  • Joint ventures: BrahMos = India–Russia; MRSAM = India–Israel (IAI); S-400 = outright Russian purchase (no JV); F414 engines = US (GE) with ToT, not a missile.

8. Current Relevance (72nd BPSC, Jul 26, 2026)

  • Op Sindoor systems: S-400 (~314 km claimed kill), Akash, counter-drone grid, BrahMos precision strikes — know which system did what.
  • 2024-26 test chain: Mission Divyastra (Agni-V MIRV, Mar 2024) → AD-2 BMD test (Jul 2024) → ET-LDHCM hypersonic (Nov 2024) → Project Kusha clearance → Military Quantum Mission framework (Jan 2026).
  • Budget hook: DRDO allocation FY 2026-27 = ₹29,100 crore (part of the ₹7.85 lakh cr defence budget).
  • Export hook: BrahMos (Philippines) and Pinaka (Armenia) are the poster systems of the ₹23,622 cr FY25 export figure; target ₹50,000 cr by 2029.
