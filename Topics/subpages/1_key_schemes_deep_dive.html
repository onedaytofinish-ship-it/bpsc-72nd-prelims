📋 Key Central Schemes — Deep Dive

Detailed breakdown of PM-KISAN, Ayushman Bharat, PMJDY, MGNREGA, PMAY, and other high-yield schemes with Bihar-specific data and exam predictions

💰 PM-KISAN (Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi)

PM-KISAN — Complete Fact Sheet HIGH YIELD

AttributeDetail
Launch date24 February 2019 (by PM Modi at Gorakhpur, UP)
MinistryMinistry of Agriculture & Farmers Welfare
TypeCentral Sector Scheme (100% Centre-funded)
Benefit₹6,000/year per family in 3 equal installments of ₹2,000 each
EligibilityAll landholding farmer families (individual or joint ownership); cultivable land
ExclusionsInstitutional landholders; former/current holders of constitutional posts; govt employees (Group A/B); PSUs employees; income tax payees; professionals (doctors, engineers, lawyers, CAs) earning >₹10,000/month
Transfer modeDBT (Direct Benefit Transfer) to Aadhaar-linked bank accounts
Installment cycle1st: Apr–Jul | 2nd: Aug–Nov | 3rd: Dec–Mar
Total beneficiaries (pan-India)~11 crore farmer families (as of 2026)
Budget allocation FY27₹60,000 crore (approx.)
🟠 Bihar-specific PM-KISAN data:
  • Bihar beneficiaries: 73.88 lakh farmers (as of 2026)
  • Total disbursed in Bihar: exceeds ₹12,000 crore cumulatively
  • Implementation: Bihar uses the PM-KISAN portal integrated with state land records (Bhulekh Bihar)
  • State complement: Bihar's own Mukhyamantri Kisan Kalyan Yojana adds an additional ₹2,000/year on top of PM-KISAN's ₹6,000 — making Bihar farmers eligible for ₹8,000/year total
PYQ PATTERN BPSC asks: (1) "PM-KISAN provides how much per year?" → ₹6,000. (2) "How many installments?" → 3. (3) "Which ministry?" → Agriculture. (4) "What type of scheme?" → Central Sector (100% Centre). (5) Bihar-specific: "How many Bihar farmers benefit?" → 73.88 lakh.
72nd PREDICTION High probability: "PM-KISAN is a _______ scheme" → Central Sector (not Centrally Sponsored). Medium probability: "What is the Bihar total for a farmer under PM-KISAN + Mukhyamantri Kisan Kalyan?" → ₹8,000/year. Low probability: "Which installment covers April–July?" → 1st installment.
🏥 Ayushman Bharat (PM-JAY)

Ayushman Bharat — Complete Fact Sheet HIGH YIELD

AttributeDetail
Launch date23 September 2018 (Jharkhand, by PM Modi)
MinistryMinistry of Health & Family Welfare
Two components(1) PM-JAY (health insurance) (2) Health & Wellness Centres (HWCs)
PM-JAY cover₹5 lakh/family/year for secondary & tertiary care
Beneficiary base~55 crore individuals (10.74 crore families from SECC 2011)
2024 expansionExtended to all senior citizens aged 70+ years (regardless of income) — Cabinet approved Sept 2024
FundingCentral Sector (60:40 Centre:State for most states; 90:10 for NE/Himalayan states)
CashlessPaperless, cashless treatment at any empanelled hospital (portable across India)
Ayushman CardAyushman Bharat Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (AB-PMJAY) card
🟠 Bihar-specific Ayushman Bharat data:
  • Bihar implementation: Bihar adopted PM-JAY; also has its own Mukhyamantri Chhatra Yogyya Yojana and Mukhyamantri Jan Kalyan Yojana
  • Bihar empanelled hospitals: 500+ hospitals (government and private)
  • Bihar beneficiaries: ~1.13 crore families (based on SECC 2011)
  • 70+ expansion: Bihar's elderly population (~60 lakh) newly eligible from 2024
PYQ PATTERN BPSC asks: (1) "Ayushman Bharat cover per family?" → ₹5 lakh/year. (2) "Which ministry?" → Health. (3) "2024 expansion?" → all senior citizens 70+. (4) "Beneficiary identification based on?" → SECC 2011. (5) "What type of treatment?" → cashless, paperless, portable.

⚒️ MGNREGA (Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Act)

MGNREGA — Complete Fact Sheet HIGH YIELD

AttributeDetail
Enacted2005 (NREG Act); renamed MGNREGA in 2009
MinistryMinistry of Rural Development
TypeCentrally Sponsored Scheme (60:40 for implementation; 100% Centre for wages)
Legal guarantee100 days of wage employment per household per year
Wage rateState-specific; revised annually; average ~₹250/day (2025-26)
Bihar wage rate (2025-26)₹222/day (unskilled workers)
Key featureGram Sabha recommends works; Gram Panchayat executes; 33% reservation for women
Wage payment modeDBT to Aadhaar-linked bank/post office accounts
🟠 Bihar MGNREGA data:
  • Bihar persondays generated (FY25): ~28 crore persondays
  • Bihar households demanded work: ~1.2 crore
  • Bihar average days of work/household: ~42 days (below 100-day guarantee — a known implementation gap)
  • Bihar women participation: ~35% (below 33% reservation target in some districts)
