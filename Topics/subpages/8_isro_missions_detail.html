Static "Firsts & Onlys" Rapid Bank

  • First satellite: Aryabhata (1975) • First Indian-built launcher success: SLV-3 (1980) • First Indian in space: Rakesh Sharma (1984) • First Indian on the ISS: Shubhanshu Shukla (2025).
  • India = 4th nation to soft-land on the Moon (2023) and 1st near the south pole; 4th to dock in space (2025); 4th with anti-satellite capability (Mission Shakti, 2019); 6th to launch own satellite (1980); 1st to reach Mars in maiden attempt (2014).
  • NavIC/IRNSS: 7 satellites (regional, India + 1,500 km); NVS = 2nd generation. GAGAN: aviation augmentation with AAI.
  • Second Indian spaceport: Kulasekarapattinam, Thoothukudi district, Tamil Nadu (foundation 2024; SSLV-focused).
  • Private firsts: Skyroot Vikram-S (Nov 2022, first private Indian rocket) • Agnikul Agnibaan SOrTeD (May 2024, first semi-cryogenic 3D-printed-engine flight) • Pixxel (hyperspectral constellation).
  • Space economy: ~$8 bn today, target $44 bn by 2033; Indian Space Policy 2023; FDI liberalisation (2024) for the space sector.
