Year Calendar of Important Days — Master Statics (Jan–Dec)
📂 Statics + Background 🎯 Fixed dates, first-observance years, theme-setters
🧭 Core Concepts — How an "Important Day" Is Born
  • UN international days/weeks/years are proclaimed by a UN General Assembly resolution (sometimes by specialised agencies — UNESCO, WHO, FAO, UNEP — for their own domains). The proclamation fixes the date permanently; the annual theme is then chosen each year by the lead agency. Example: UNGA proclaimed the International Day of Yoga in 2014 (resolution piloted by India, adopted by consensus); the Ministry of Ayush announces each year's theme.
  • National days (India) are declared by the Union Government (gazette notification / cabinet decision) — e.g., Constitution Day (2015), National Space Day (2023), Samvidhan Hatya Diwas (2024), Good Governance Day (2014).
  • Theme-issuing bodies (matching bank): UNEP → World Environment Day • WHO → World Health Day • UN-Water → World Water Day • UNESCO → International Literacy Day • FAO → World Food Day • IOC → Olympic Day • EARTHDAY.ORG (NGO) → Earth Day • ECI → National Voters' Day • DST → National Science Day • Ministry of Ayush → IDY • IEI → Engineers' Day.
  • BPSC pattern: (1) exact date of a day ("Ozone day is celebrated on:" — 71st); (2) year-specific theme ("theme of International Olympic Day 2024" — 70th); (3) day↔date or day↔theme matching; (4) "which pair is incorrect" traps using adjacent-year themes.

🧠 Mnemonic & Trap Bank
🧠 The Twin-Date Traps BPSC Loves
• 21 June = Yoga vs 21 December = Meditation + Basketball
• 16 September = Ozone vs 16 October = Food
• 23 June = Olympic vs 23 October = Snow Leopard
• 25 January = Voters' vs 25 June = Samvidhan Hatya vs 25 December = Good Governance
• 22 March = Water + Bihar Diwas vs 22 April = Earth vs 22 December = Mathematics
  • First-observance years worth memorising: Earth Day 1970 • WED 1973 • Women's Day (UN) 1975 • NVD 2011 • Sports Day 2012 • IDY 2015 • Constitution Day 2015 • Unity Day 2014 • Space Day 2024 • Meditation Day 2024 • Football/Potato Days 2024 • Snow Leopard Day 2024 • Basketball Day 2023 • Glacier Day 2025.
  • Theme-setter traps: Earth Day's theme comes from EARTHDAY.ORG (an NGO), not UNEP; Olympic Day's theme from the IOC; NVD's theme from the ECI. BPSC can ask "who announces the theme".
  • Ordinal arithmetic: Nth edition in year Y = Y − (first year) + 1. Republic Day: 2026 − 1950 + 1 = 77th. Independence Day: 2025 − 1947 + 1 = 79th. UN Day: 2025 − 1945 = 80th (anniversaries don't add 1).
