Ganga Bridges of Bihar — Master Table, Engineering & Statics
🌉 Core Concepts + Statics 📅 Window: Jan 2025 – 15 Jul 2026 🎯 Feeds bridge/infra MCQs
🧭 Core Concept — Why Ganga Crossings Define Bihar
  • The Ganga bisects Bihar into a flood-prone, densely populated north (Tirhut, Kosi, Seemanchal) and a south (Magadh, Anga) holding Patna and most industry. Until the 1980s the river had almost no permanent crossings inside Bihar — every bridge since has been a state-defining event.
  • Diara (chars) — the riverine islands and sandbars between the Ganga's shifting channels — house lakhs of people (e.g., Raghopur Diara, Vaishali) historically dependent on boats. The Kacchi Dargah–Bidupur bridge is Raghopur's first fast, all-weather link to Patna (~5 minutes).
  • Exam lens: BPSC tests name ↔ river stretch ↔ type ↔ year, plus superlatives ("longest extra-dosed", "rail-cum-road") and collapse/pipeline news.

⚙️ Bridge-Type Statics — Extra-Dosed vs Cable-Stayed
  • Extra-dosed bridge: a hybrid of a prestressed box-girder and a cable-stayed bridge — short towers ("pylons") with shallow-angle stay cables that prestress the girder more than they support it. Cheaper and stiffer than full cable-stayed for medium spans; ideal for wide, shallow navigation channels like the Ganga's.
  • Cable-stayed bridge: tall pylons, steep fan/harp cables carry the deck directly (e.g., Bandra–Worli Sea Link; the Aguwani–Sultanganj design).
  • Rail-cum-road bridge: combined rail deck + roadway on one structure (Digha–Sonpur, 2016) — Bihar's first of its kind over the Ganga.
  • Executing agencies to pair: BSRDCL (Bihar State Road Development Corporation) — Kacchi Dargah–Bidupur; NHAI — Aunta–Simaria (NH-31) and national-highway works; RVNL/Indian Railways — rail-cum-road structures.
How BPSC frames it: superlative swaps ("longest" vs "widest" extra-dosed), year/type matching (1982 ↔ 2016 ↔ 2025), and "which agency built it" (BSRDCL vs NHAI). Aunta–Simaria's 22 Aug 2025 inauguration was part of the PM's Gaya event — but the bridge itself links Mokama–Begusarai, not Gaya town.
📰 Current Relevance (Jan 2025 – 15 Jul 2026)
  • 23 Jun 2025: Kacchi Dargah–Bidupur Phase-1 inaugurated by CM Nitish Kumar (~85% complete at opening; full commissioning followed).
  • 22 Aug 2025: PM Modi inaugurated the Aunta–Simaria project and the 4-lane Bakhtiyarpur–Mokama NH-31 section (~₹1,900 cr) at the ~₹13,000-crore Gaya event.
  • Expressway synergy: the Amas–Darbhanga Expressway (NH-119D, Dec 2026 target) will route its Ganga crossing over the Kacchi Dargah–Bidupur bridge — one bridge, two headlines.
  • Waterway angle: NW-1 (Haldia–Prayagraj on the Ganga) gained the Kalughat intermodal terminal (Sonepur, Saran) with cargo ops from ~Nov 2025; Gaighat (Patna) already functional.
  • Caution list (as of 15 Jul 2026): Aguwani–Sultanganj — rebuild, no completion; several proposed crossings (e.g., additional north–south links) remain at planning/approval stage — treat "opened" options for them as traps.
