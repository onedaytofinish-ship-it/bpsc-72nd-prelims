🔶 Bihar Connection — Deep Dive
3 Mahajanapadas in Bihar:
  • Magadha: most powerful. Capital: Rajgir (surrounded by 5 hills) → Pataliputra (Ganga/Son/Punpun). Key rulers: Bimbisara (founder Haryanka, patron of Buddhism), Ajatashatru (conquered Vajji+Kosala), Mahapadma Nanda ('destroyer of Kshatriyas').
  • Vajji: republic (gana-sangha). Capital: Vaishali (Muzaffarpur district). Confederacy of clans (Licchavi). One of world's earliest republics. Birthplace of Mahavira. Second Buddhist Council (383 BCE). Conquered by Ajatashatru (16-year war).
  • Anga: capital: Champa (Bhagalpur). Major Ganga port. Conquered by Bimbisara (first Magadhan expansion).
Buddhist Councils (all first three in Bihar):
  • First: Rajgir (483 BCE, Saptaparni cave, Ajatashatru)
  • Second: Vaishali (383 BCE, Kalashoka)
  • Third: Pataliputra (250 BCE, Ashoka)
🎯 Exam Predictions
P1 16 Mahajanapadas + 4 powerful (HIGH): 16 total. 4 powerful: Magadha, Kosala, Vatsa, Avanti. 3 in Bihar: Magadha, Vajji, Anga.
P2 Magadha dynasties (HIGH): Haryanka (Bimbisara, Ajatashatru) → Shishunaga → Nanda (Mahapadma) → Maurya. HSNM. Capital: Rajgir → Pataliputra.
P3 Bihar — 3 Mahajanapadas (HIGH): Magadha (Rajgir/Pataliputra), Vajji (Vaishali, republic), Anga (Champa). Bihar = heartland of Mahajanapadas + Buddhism + Jainism.
P4 Vajji republic (MEDIUM): Gana-sangha. Vaishali. Licchavi. Mahavira's birthplace. Buddha praised democracy. Conquered by Ajatashatru (16-year war).
P5 Buddhist Councils (MEDIUM): First (Rajgir, 483, Ajatashatru), Second (Vaishali, 383, Kalashoka), Third (Pataliputra, 250, Ashoka). All in Bihar!
P6 Key rulers (LOW): Bimbisara (founder, Buddhism, Anga), Ajatashatru (Vajji+Kosala, First Council), Mahapadma Nanda (destroyer of Kshatriyas, standing army).
P7 Nandas → Mauryas (LOW): Mahapadma Nanda (Shudra). Dhana Nanda (last). Chandragupta Maurya (322 BCE) with Chanakya. First pan-Indian empire.
