Bihar's Rulers: Premiers → CMs (1937–2026) + Governors + Appointment Articles
1. Core Concept — The Appointment Articles (BPSC's favourite matching bank)
PostArticleAppointed By / Key Rule
GovernorArt 155 (appointment), Art 156 (pleasure)President of India; holds office during the President's pleasure; usual term 5 years; must be 35+, citizen, not an MP/MLA
Chief Minister & Council of MinistersArt 164; CoM aids & advises (Art 163)CM appointed by the Governor; ministers hold office during the Governor's pleasure; CoM collectively responsible to the Assembly (Art 164(2)); a non-member can be minister for max 6 months (Art 164(4))
HC Chief Justice / JudgesArt 217(1) (appointment), Art 219 (oath), Art 223 (Acting CJ)Appointed by the President after consultation (collegium); Acting CJ when the office is vacant or the CJ is unable to act
State PSC Chairman & MembersArt 316 (appointment by Governor); Art 317 (removal only by President on SC reference)Security of tenure — can be removed only for misbehaviour after a Supreme Court inquiry; chairman barred from further employment under GoI/state after term
State Election CommissionerArt 243KAppointed by the Governor; removal only in the manner of a High Court judge
Advocate GeneralArt 165Appointed by the Governor; holds office during the Governor's pleasure; state's top law officer
Speaker / Dy Speaker; Council ChairmanArt 178 / 182Chosen by the respective House from among its members
🧠 Mnemonic — "G-155, C-164, J-217, P-316, E-243K"
Governor 155 • CM 164 • Judge 217 • State PSC 316 • Election Commissioner 243K. And removals: PSC chairman — only President after SC inquiry (Art 317); SEC — like an HC judge.
