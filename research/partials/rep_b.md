## 1. World Bank Business Ready (B-READY) — successor to Ease of Doing Business

| Field | Detail |
|---|---|
| Full name | Business Ready (B-READY) report |
| Publisher | World Bank Group |
| Latest edition | **B-READY 2025**, released 29–30 Dec 2025 — interim 2nd edition of a 3-year rollout (2024: 50 economies → 2025: 101 economies → 2026: 160+ economies; full baseline ~180 by 2027) |
| India status | **India is NOT in B-READY 2024 or 2025.** India is listed in the **B-READY 2026** cohort (62 additional economies), i.e., India's first scores come with the 2026 report (expected late 2026/2027) |
| Methodology | 3 pillars: Regulatory Framework, Public Services, Operational Efficiency; 10 business-life-cycle topics; data from ~5,000 experts + 58,000 firm surveys; 2025 edn has a "jobs perspective" |

**EoDB framing for BPSC:** The old **Doing Business / Ease of Doing Business** report was **discontinued — the last edition was Doing Business 2020** (published Oct 2019); the World Bank formally announced discontinuation on 16 Sep 2021 after data-irregularity findings. B-READY is its replacement. (India's last EoDB rank: 63rd of 190, Doing Business 2020.)

Sources: [World Bank B-READY page](https://www.worldbank.org/en/businessready) | [B-READY covered economies — India under 2026](https://www.worldbank.org/en/businessready/about-us/covered-economies) | [WB reproducibility package, B-READY 2025, 101 economies](https://reproducibility.worldbank.org/index.php/catalog/study/FR_WLD_2025_523) | [WBG Institute: 2025 interim edn, rollout 2024–26](https://www.wbginstitute.org/topic/315/business-ready) | [Vietnam News: 101 economies, Dec 2025 release, 160+ by 2026](https://vietnamnews.vn/economy/1732986/việt-nam-ranks-among-top-performers-in-world-banks-business-ready-2025-report.html)
