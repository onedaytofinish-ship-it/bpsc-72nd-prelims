### 6.2 65% quota law → Patna HC → Supreme Court

## 7. STATICS (evergreen pair-ups examiners use)
- **State basics:** Bihar separated from Bengal **22 Mar 1912** (Bihar Diwas); Jharkhand carved out 15 Nov 2000; capital Patna; 38 districts, 9 divisions; area 94,163 km²; borders Nepal (N), UP (W), Jharkhand (S), WB (E).
- **Legislature:** 243 Vidhan Sabha + 75 Vidhan Parishad seats; 40 Lok Sabha + 16 Rajya Sabha seats; Raj Bhavan renamed **"Lok Bhavan"**.
- **CM records:** 1st CM Sri Krishna Sinha (1946–61); 1st woman CM Rabri Devi; shortest CM Satish Prasad Singh (5 days); President's Rule 8 times; Nitish Kumar = longest-serving (10 oaths, ~20 yrs, till 14 Apr 2026); Samrat Chaudhary = 24th CM & first BJP CM (15 Apr 2026).
- **Saat Nischay arc:** Part-1 (2015–20): bijli-sadak-pani, shauchalay, aarthik hal/yuvaon ko bal, aarogya; Part-2 (2020–25): yuva shakti, mahila sashaktikaran, krishi & har khet ko sinchai, har ghar nal ka jal, ghar tak pakki sadak, udyog/rozgar themes (₹5,972 cr in 2025-26); **Part-3 (2025–30)**: double per-capita income, industrialisation, farmer income, education/health, new cities, ease of living.
- **JEEViKA/BRLPS:** est. 2006, World Bank-supported; SHG federation model; ~1.4+ crore rural households mobilised (approx; static).
- **MMRY distinction:** ₹10,000 grant (Bihar MMRY, Sept 2025) vs central PM Vishwakarma/PMEGP loans — exams love this confusion.
- **Caste survey firsts:** Bihar = first state since 1931 to enumerate all castes (2023); triggered national caste-census demand → Census 2027 caste enumeration.
- **Quota ceiling jurisprudence:** Indra Sawhney (1992) 50% cap; 103rd Amendment (10% EWS, upheld in *Janhit Abhiyan* 2022); Bihar's 65% bid struck (Patna HC 2024).
- **16th FC:** Bihar devolution 9.95% (2026-31) — 2nd highest after UP (17.62%).
