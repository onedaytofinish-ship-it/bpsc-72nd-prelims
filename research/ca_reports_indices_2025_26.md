# Reports & Indices Research Cache — 2025–26

Retrieved: 17 July 2026. Use the edition/year shown; rankings are not automatically comparable across editions unless the publisher says so.

## India: high-yield current figures

| Report / index | Publisher | Current exam fact |
|---|---|---|
| Human Development Report 2025 | UNDP | India: rank **130/193**, HDI **0.685** (2023 data), medium human development; life expectancy **72.0** years. |
| Global Hunger Index 2025 | Concern Worldwide & Welthungerhilfe | India: **102/123**, score **25.8**, “serious”. Components: undernourishment 12.0%, child wasting 18.7%, stunting 32.9%, under-five mortality 2.8%. The publisher says rankings/scores are comparable only within the same edition. |
| Global Gender Gap Report 2025 | World Economic Forum | India: **131/148**, parity score **64.4%**. |
| Global Innovation Index 2025 | WIPO | India: **38/139**, first among lower-middle-income economies and Central/Southern Asia; input rank 52, output rank 32. |
| World Happiness Report 2026 | Wellbeing Research Centre (University of Oxford), Gallup & UN SDSN | India: **116**, score 4.536. Use the report year—2025 rank was 118, so do not mix editions. |
| SDG India Index 2023–24 | NITI Aayog | India score **71**; Kerala and Uttarakhand topped states at **79**. Index assesses 16 SDGs (Goal 17 is not ranked). |

## Bihar: national-report anchors

| Report | Bihar fact |
|---|---|
| SDG India Index 2023–24 (NITI Aayog) | **57**, lowest-ranked state; “Performer” band (50–64). Its low scores included SDG 2 Zero Hunger **24** and SDG 4 Quality Education **32**. |
| National MPI Progress Review 2023 (NITI Aayog; NFHS-5 2019–21) | Multidimensionally poor headcount: **33.76%**, down from **51.89%** (NFHS-4, 2015–16); intensity **47.40%**, MPI **0.160**. Rural H=36.95%; urban H=16.67%. |
| Multidimensional Poverty in India since 2005–06 (NITI Aayog, 2024) | Bihar’s 2022–23 headcount is an **extrapolated estimate: 26.59%**; 377.09 lakh estimated people escaped multidimensional poverty in 2013–14 to 2022–23. Do not label it a direct survey result. |

## Guardrails

- HDI 2025 is based on 2023 data; it is not a 2025 outcome measurement.
- GHI methodology, country set and source data change: do not compare a rank across different editions.
- Bihar’s MPI 2022–23 figure is extrapolated; the directly observed NFHS-5 period is 2019–21.
